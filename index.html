March 24th 2023

I got to be a part of an event called AWS GameDay. The event was hosted by Amazon in one of their buildings in Manhattan. The building is called Amazon JFK 14 and it was so much fun. I got the opportunity to work with other students and work together on solving problems on a website utilizing the AWS services. The services we were able to use was AWS EC2, VPC, S3, WAF, and RDS. I knew what they were but never really used them before. I was only familiar with EC2 because I did use it before. It was a friendly competition among the groups. I honestly had so much fun interacting and talking with the students and staff there. I learned so much from them. It made me realize working with others and learning from them and talking about our passions is truly the happiest feeling for me. I want to experience life and work like that everyday. I was filled with excitement and filled with curiousity on wanting to keep learning and learning from people. I kept nonstop talking about code and explaining my process on how I was able to understand JavaScript. Learning is so much fun to me. I don't regret going to this event one bit. It was truly the best decision I ever made. I'm glad I recieved an invitation.
